How it works

Three steps from external drive to curated shortlist. No phone app required.

1

Add a folder

Open PicTinder on your Mac or PC and point it at any folder — local or external drive. It scans RAW, HEIC, JPEG, and video.

2

Scan the QR code

PicTinder shows a QR code. Scan it with any phone on the same Wi-Fi — no app install, just a browser.

3

Swipe to curate

Swipe right to keep, left to skip. Multiple people can review at the same time. Export your shortlist when done.

Everything you need to cull fast

Built for wedding photographers, creative teams, and anyone with too many photos.

Multi-device collaboration

Shared mode aggregates votes from all reviewers. Distributed mode splits the workload so faster swipers get more items.

RAW, HEIC & video

Handles NEF, ARW, CR2, CR3, DNG, RAF, HEIC, and video formats including MOV, AVI, and MKV with automatic transcoding.

Face recognition

Faces are detected and highlighted automatically. Name people and filter your feed by person for targeted review.

Smart filters

Filter your review feed by file type, subfolder path, or detected person. Each reviewer can pick different filters to divide and conquer.

Google Drive export

Upload your shortlist to Google Drive. Choose to duplicate across accounts or distribute by free space. Pause, resume, retry.

Originals stay safe

No files are ever moved, deleted, or overwritten. All state lives in a local database. Your originals remain exactly where they are.
