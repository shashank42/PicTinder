Getting started
  1. Scan the QR code on the desktop app, or open in your browser. Invite friends, family, or your team.
  2. Create an album and choose a mode — split photos across devices or let everyone see all of them.
  3. Swipe left & right (or use arrow keys) to browse through photos and videos.
  4. Filter your feed by file type, folder, or person to focus on what matters.
  5. Swipe up to select, swipe down to reject. Build your shortlist in seconds.
  6. Identify people — the app detects faces and lets you name friends & family.
  7. Share to Google Drive — upload selected photos to each person’s account automatically.
