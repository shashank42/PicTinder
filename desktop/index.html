Getting started
  1. Scan the QR code on your phone, or share the link. Invite friends, family, or your team.
  2. Create an album and choose a mode — split photos or let everyone see all.
  3. Swipe left & right to browse photos and videos.
  4. Filter your feed by file type, folder, or person.
  5. Swipe up to select, down to reject. Build your shortlist fast.
  6. Identify people — faces are detected and you can name them.
  7. Share to Google Drive — upload selected photos to each person.
